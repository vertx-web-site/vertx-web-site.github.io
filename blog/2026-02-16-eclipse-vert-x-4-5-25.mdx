We are extremely pleased to announce that Eclipse Vert.x version 4.5.25 has been released.

Since the release of Vert.x 4.5.24, quite a few bugs have been reported. We would like to thank you all for reporting these issues.

The [4.5.25 release notes](https://github.com/vert-x3/wiki/wiki/4.5.25-Release-Notes) as well as
[deprecations and breaking changes](https://github.com/vert-x3/wiki/wiki/4.5.25-Deprecations-and-breaking-changes)
 can be found on the wiki.

The release artifacts have been deployed to [Maven Central](https://search.maven.org/search?q=g:io.vertx%20AND%20v:4.5.25).

Vert.x follows a 2 years policy after a major release, as consequence Vert.x remains supported until April 2027 and get
bug fixes, you should consider [upgrading](https://vertx.io/blog/from-vert-x-4-to-vert-x-5/) your application to Vert.x 5.

That's it! Happy coding and see you soon on our user or dev [channels](https://vertx.io/channels).
